About POMBOX

POMBOX is a molecular and synthetic biology toolkit dedicated to build single and multi-gene plasmids in S. pombe using GoldeGate.
POMBOX proposes 40 characterized parts, promoters, terminators, integration vectors, and 2 ready-to-use backbone vectors.
POMBOX is compatible with the YeastToolKit and uses its grammar.
