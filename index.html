About POMBOX

POMBOX is a molecular and synthetic biology toolkit dedicated to construct single and multi-gene plasmids in S. pombe in streamlined and efficient manner. POMBOX propose 40 characterized parts and 2 ready to use backbone vectore. POMBOX is compatible with the YeastToolKit and uses his grammar.
